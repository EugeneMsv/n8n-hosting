What is n8n?

The Workflow Automation Tool

n8n is a fair-code, node-based workflow automation tool. It allows you to build complex automations faster by connecting disparate apps and services.

  • Fair-code: Source available and free for internal use.
  • Self-Hostable: Complete control over your data and infrastructure.
  • Developer Friendly: Go beyond simple integrations with custom JavaScript/Python.

Why n8n?

Simple

Intuitive node-based editor lets you drag, drop, and connect services to visualize complex logic.

Flexible enough

Capability to inject code directly into nodes for advanced data manipulation.

Community and Docs

130K weekly visitors and 3K weekly contributors on Reddit. 20 time more then for competitors like Zapier or Make.
